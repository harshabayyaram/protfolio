Academic Projects


Radio Frequency Based Identification attendance and student monitoring system with SMS alerts

The RF-based identification attendance and student monitoring system with SMS alerts using Arduino and GSM is an advanced solution that automates attendance tracking in educational institutions. By utilizing RF technology and Arduino boards, students can be uniquely identified through RFID cards or tags, ensuring accuracy and efficiency. The system enables real-time monitoring of attendance data, which can be instantly updated and accessed by teachers, administrators, and parents. Additionally, SMS alerts can be sent to notify parents or guardians about student absences, facilitating timely communication and intervention. This comprehensive system improves accountability, reduces manual effort, and enhances the overall educational experience.


Automatic fire detection and control with call and sms alerts to fire department and police department

The Automatic Fire Detection and Control system with call and SMS alerts to fire and police departments, using Arduino and GSM technology, is a cutting-edge solution designed to enhance fire safety and emergency response. This system utilizes sensors to detect smoke or rapid changes in temperature, triggering an immediate alert. By integrating Arduino and GSM modules, the system automatically notifies the fire and police departments via phone calls and SMS alerts, providing them with crucial information about the location and severity of the fire. This real-time communication allows for swift and coordinated emergency response, reducing the risk of property damage and potential harm to human life. The integration of Arduino and GSM technology ensures the system's reliability, scalability, and ease of implementation, making it an invaluable tool in mitigating fire hazards and improving public safety.
